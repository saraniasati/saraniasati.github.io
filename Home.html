A little about me

Welcome to my UX Design portfolio website! I'm thrilled to have the opportunity to share my passion for creating inclusive and accessible digital products with you.

My name is Sara, and I bring a diverse range of interests and experiences to my journey as a UX designer.

With a background in cognitive systems, a multidisciplinary program at the University of British Columbia that encompasses the study of cognitive systems and their interaction with the environment from psychology, computer science, linguistics, and the philosophy perspectives, I have developed a comprehensive understanding of human psychology and behavior, as well as the intricate interaction between humans and computers. This interdisciplinary foundation has provided me with a holistic approach to UX design, enabling me to consider the cognitive, emotional, and behavioral aspects of user experiences.

Currently, I am further enhancing my knowledge and skills through my enrollment in the UX Design program at BrainStation. This program has been instrumental in deepening my understanding of user experience (UX) and user interface (UI) design, providing me with comprehensive learning experiences ranging from fundamental principles to hands-on projects.

One of the unique aspects of this program is its remote setting, which has allowed me to develop the ability to collaborate effectively in virtual teams. Working on multiple projects, both individually and as part of a group, has provided me with valuable practical experience in applying UX/UI design principles to real-world scenarios. These hands-on experiences have helped me hone my skills in conducting user research, creating wireframes and prototypes, and iterating designs based on user feedback.

Navigating the remote learning environment has also taught me the importance of clear communication and effective collaboration tools. I have gained proficiency in leveraging remote collaboration platforms to foster productive teamwork and ensure seamless project execution.

As a designer, I am deeply committed to inclusive design principles. Growing up in the Middle East, specifically Iran, I witnessed the struggles people faced when using English-heavy digital products. This experience ignited my passion for making language accessible products and finding ways to create intuitive interfaces that transcend language barriers. I firmly believe that technology should be inclusive and empower individuals from all backgrounds to engage with it effortlessly.

Problem-solving and uncovering patterns in cognitive agent interactions with technology fuel my creativity. I am constantly seeking out new challenges and opportunities to push the boundaries of design, whether it's through researching user behaviors, conducting usability testing, or prototyping innovative solutions.

Outside of my academic and professional pursuits, I find great fulfillment as the host of the Farsi-language podcast, Mind Master. This platform allows me to give back to the community in which I was born and raised by sharing valuable insights from various multidisciplinary fields and delving into the intricacies of the human mind. By podcasting in Farsi, my mother tongue, I can connect with and contribute to the Persian-speaking community, fostering a sense of unity and knowledge-sharing. This experience has not only allowed me to share my passion and expertise but has also created meaningful connections with like-minded individuals, further strengthening the sense of community we share.

In my free time, I indulge in my love for nature and staying active. Hiking, yoga, and meditation are activities that provide me with clarity, helping me recharge my energy and stimulate my creativity. I am an avid learner, always seeking new styles of self-improvement and personal growth.

I believe in the power of human connection and constantly strive to make a positive impact in the world. Through my work as a UX designer, I aim to bridge the gap between people and technology, creating meaningful and accessible experiences for all. Thank you for visiting my portfolio website, and I look forward to connecting with you and collaborating on exciting projects that shape the future of UX design.
